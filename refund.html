Refund Policy

EaseLaw.in provides digital legal-drafting services. Since all services are instantly accessible upon purchase, most payments are non-refundable.

Digital services become accessible immediately after purchase. Therefore, refunds are restricted except in limited exceptional circumstances.

A. Non-Refundable Services

Payments cannot be refunded for:

  • AI-generated drafts
  • OCR / translation services
  • Subscription plans
  • Credits purchased
  • User mistakes in entered details
  • Dissatisfaction with AI output
  • Change of mind
  • Unused subscription period

B. Refunds Allowed (Exceptional Cases Only)

Refunds may be granted only for:

  • Accidental duplicate payment
  • Incorrect payment made to EaseLaw by mistake
  • Technical failure where service was NOT delivered
To request a refund:

📩 Email: support@easelaw.in

Please provide:
• Name
• Phone Number
• Email Address
• Order ID
• Payment Proof
• Reason for refund request

Refund Processing Time

Refund requests are reviewed by our support team and may take approximately 7–14 business days for processing.

Refund approval is completely at the discretion of EaseLaw.in.

C. No Cash Refunds

Refunds are issued only through the original payment method used during the transaction (Razorpay / PayU).

EaseLaw.in reserves the right to deny refund requests that do not satisfy the eligibility conditions mentioned above.
